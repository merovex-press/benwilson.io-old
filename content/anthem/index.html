--- author: Ben Wilson title: Anthem date: "2021-04-29T04:47:33-04:00" ---

I chose my thoughts. I know that doing my best starts with thinking my best. Like laying down a path for an adventure, these thoughts set the course for my actions.

I am confident in what I think matters. I am excited to see what happens next. I am disciplined and dedicated to stick with it.

Here are 10 things I know:

  1. Today is brand new. Tomorrow is, too.
  2. I have a gift worth giving (I have a voice and a right to be heard)
  3. The only person standing in my way is me. I quit doing that yesterday
  4. God is my boss, and he is the best boss.
  5. Winning is contagious. When I help others win, I win, too.
  6. Feeling uncomfortable is just a sign that my old comfort zone is having a hard time keeping up with me.
  7. Momentum is messy. Progress is the goal. Complexity the bridge. Simplicity the destination.
  8. All things work together for good to those who love God.
  9. I am my biggest fan.
  10. The best response to obstacles is to do it anyway.

I've pulled the slingshot back. I am not leaving this room. I am launching from it, ready for a day full of untold opportunities. I've packed honesty, generosity, laughter and bravery for the road ahead.

What a day. The best part is I left myself a lot of fun things to work on tomorrow. WHen my head hits that pillow, I'm off the clock, storing up energy and excitement for a brand-new day.
