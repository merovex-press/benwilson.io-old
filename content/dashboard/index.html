Want to use this Dashboard?

I did not expect a simple dashboard to be of interest to my fellow authors. It was just something I cobbled together to motivate myself. However, if there is enough interest, I might carve some writing time to make this available to a wider audience. My first inkling was to put a MailerLite subscribe form and invite you to join a waitlist. A little too impersonal at this stage.

I am keenly interested in what you like about it. What features do you find most appealing? What's missing? Would you embed this dashboard in your site? If so, are you using Wordpress or another content management tool? Or would you prefer this as a standalone site you can refer to?

Answers to these questions and any other feedback are welcome. They help give me a better idea of how to approach serving your interest. Email me directly at with your feedback.

If you email me, I will manually add you to a mailing list so I can share status easily. If you don't want to join the mailing list, just mention that in your email to me.
